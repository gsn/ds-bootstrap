How to earn awards

Spend $50 on Pet products when using your Rewards card and get $5 OFF your next pet purchase.

How to redeem current awards

$5 coupon printed after $50 in Pet product purchases while using Rewards card has been reached. Coupon can be redeemed on next Pet item purchased in conjunction with usage of your Rewards card.

You are automatically entered into the Country Mart Pet Club when you use your Rewards card on the purchase of a pet item.

Lunch Club

Club Balance

  • Lunch Club awards earned but not yet used. {{getClubValue('kccm_lunch_club_awards_due', false)}}
  • Number of lunches purchased. {{getClubValue('kccm_lunch_club_awards_received', false)}}
  • Lunch Club awards used. {{getClubValue('kccm_lunch_club_item_counter', false)}}

How to earn awards

Buy 12 lunches in the deli, get the 13th free when using your Rewards card.

How to redeem current awards

Purchase 12 lunches from the deli, having scanned your Rewards card each time and a coupon will print on the receipt of the 12th lunch purchased for a free lunch from the deli on your next visit.

Please Note

You are automatically entered into the lunch club when swiping your Rewards card while making a purchase of a lunch from the deli at Bi-Lo, Ottawa & Sabetha Country Marts.

  • Number of discounts issued in total. {{getClubValue('cm_136_salad_discounts_issued', false)}}

How to earn awards

Buy 10 salads at the deli and use your Rewards card during your purchase to receive a free salad on your next visit.

A free salad is automatically added to your Rewards card after the 10th salad purchase and will be redeemed on the next salad transaction.

Please Note

You are automatically entered into the salad club when salads are purchased using your Rewards card at Bi-Lo & Ottawa Country Mart locations.
