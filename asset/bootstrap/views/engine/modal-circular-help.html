Reading circulars


ads flyer view

Hover over or tap an item to show the “add item” option. Click on the “add item” button that appears, to add that particular item to your shopping list. To change pages, simply click the numbered buttons or arrow at the top/bottom of the circular and this will allow you to navigate throughout the entire weekly ad. With a touch-enabled device, swipe left or right to switch between pages.

Non-interactive flyer will denote as: * (meaning that it is an image only circular page)


Grid grid view

Use the search feature to find specific items. Hover over the item to see the full description and the zoom option for the product image. Click “add item” to add the particular product to your shopping list.


text text-only view

All items are listed by department/category. This view loads faster because it doesn’t show the product image. It allows you to add the particular product by simply clicking the “add item” button on the right side of the item description.
